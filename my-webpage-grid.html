Beijing

This is the capital city of China. It has a great and rich history, and it is inhabited by diverse people, local and foreign. I have had the great fortune of visiting this beautiful place for a year and I feel nostalgic whenever I am triggered by these captured memories and phrases.

Memorable phrases in Mandarin

Phrase Pin-yin (Pronounciation) Translation
#1 你好！ Nǐhǎo (Nee haow) Hello!
#2 你好吗？ Nǐhǎo ma? (Nee haow ma) How are you?
#3 很好 Hěn hǎo (Hun haow) Very Good!
